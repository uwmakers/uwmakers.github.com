Tim Vieregge


Biography

Tim Vieregge is a first year Computer Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. He is originally a C++ programmer and automated robotics enthusiast. Whilst working on projects, he has been introduced to embeded systems programming and design, mechanical designe, sensor integration, high level event managment and interprocess communication.
